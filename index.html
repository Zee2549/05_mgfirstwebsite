Lemon Drizzle Cake Recipe

A Traditional Family Favorite

About the Recipe:

Preparation Time: 15 mins Cooking Time: 45 mins Level of difficulty: Easy Number of servings: 6

Ingredients:

  1. 225g unsalted butter
  2. 180g caster sugar
  3. 4 eggs
  4. 225g self-raising flour
  5. 1 lemon, zested
  6. 2 lemons, juiced
  7. 65g caster sugar

Recipe for the cake:

Step 1 Pre-heat the oven to 180¬∞C.
Step 2 Beat together the butter and caster sugar until creamy. Add the eggs one at a time, slowly mixing through.
Step 3 Sift in the self-raising flour, then add the lemon zest and mix until well combined.
Step 4 Line a loaf tin (8 x 21cm) with greaseproof paper, then pour in the mixture and level the top with a spoon.
Step 5 Bake for 45 to 50 minutes.

Recipe for the lemon drizzle:

Step 1 Mix the lemon juice and caster sugar to make the drizzle.
Step 2 Prick the cake all over with a fork, then pour over the drizzle ‚Äì Let the juice sink in. The sugar will form a sweet, crisp topping.
Step 3 Leave the cake in the tin until completely cool before serving.
